Raw Milk Intake and Final Customer Delivery

I built from scratch a complete ERPNext case study in a Linux environment, focusing on full traceability in dairy production. The project covers the entire workflow — from raw milk intake to final delivery — including Purchasing, Quality Control, Manufacturing, and CRM, and is showcased through a custom demo website developed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
